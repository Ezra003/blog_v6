## Where to Search

- Immobilienscout24, Immowelt, WG-Gesucht (for flatshares), eBay Kleinanzeigen
- Facebook groups and local expat forums

## Required Documents

- Passport/ID
- Proof of income (salary slips, employment contract)
- SCHUFA credit report
- Mieterselbstauskunft (self-disclosure form)
- Previous landlord reference (if available)

## The Rental Process

1. Search and apply online
2. Attend viewings (bring your documents)
3. Submit your application
4. If accepted, sign the rental contract (Mietvertrag)
5. Pay the deposit (up to 3 months’ rent)

## Tenant Rights & Deposit

- Deposits must be held in a separate account
- Tenants have strong rights; evictions are rare
- Rent increases are regulated

## Avoiding Scams

- Never pay cash or via Western Union
- Beware of deals that seem too good to be true
- Always see the apartment in person before paying
